# Patient Login Guide - HealthMR

## How Patients Login

### Step 1: Registration
1. Visit the website homepage
2. Click "Register" or "Get Started"
3. Fill in the registration form with:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number
   - National Identification Number (NIN)
   - Date of Birth
   - Gender
   - Address
4. Submit the form
5. You will receive a **HealthMR ID** (e.g., HMR-2025-000001)
6. **IMPORTANT**: Save this HealthMR ID - you need it to login!

### Step 2: Account Activation
After registration, your account needs to be activated by hospital staff. This creates your login credentials automatically using:
- **Email**: The email you provided
- **Password**: Your NIN + "@HealthMR" (e.g., if NIN is 12345678901, password is 12345678901@HealthMR)

### Step 3: Login
1. Go to the Patient Login page
2. Enter your **HealthMR ID** (from registration)
3. Enter your **NIN** (11-digit number)
4. Click "Login"
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Login Credentials

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid HealthMR ID or NIN"
- Check that your HealthMR ID is correct (case-sensitive)
- Verify your NIN is exactly 11 digits
- Make sure you registered first

### "Account Setup Required"
- Your account hasn't been activated by hospital staff yet
- Contact the hospital admin to complete setup
- This usually happens within 24 hours of registration

### "Authentication Error"
- Your account credentials may not be set up correctly
- Contact hospital support for assistance

### Dashboard shows "Loading..."
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache and try again
- If problem persists, contact support

## Security Tips

1. **Keep your NIN private** - It's used as part of your password
2. **Don't share your HealthMR ID** with unauthorized persons
3. **Logout after each session** - Click the Logout button
4. **Use a secure device** - Don't login on public computers
